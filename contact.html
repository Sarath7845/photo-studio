Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photography sessions and booking process.

We recommend booking your newborn session during your second or third trimester to secure your spot. Newborn sessions are ideally scheduled within the first 5-14 days after birth when babies are sleepiest and most flexible. We understand that babies rarely arrive on their due date, so we keep our schedule flexible around your expected due date.

Session lengths vary depending on the type:
- Newborn sessions: 3-4 hours (including feeding and soothing breaks)
- Maternity sessions: 1-2 hours
- Milestone sessions: 1-2 hours
- Family sessions: 1-2 hours
We never rush our sessions and ensure we have enough time to capture beautiful images.

For newborn sessions, we provide all props, wraps, outfits, and accessories. You're welcome to bring special items like a family heirloom or meaningful toy to incorporate. For other sessions, we'll provide guidance on what to wear and bring based on the theme and location of your shoot. We recommend bringing snacks, drinks, and any comfort items for children.

You'll receive a sneak peek of 2-3 images within 48 hours of your session. The full gallery is typically ready within 2-3 weeks. Each image is carefully hand-edited to ensure the highest quality. Rush processing is available for an additional fee if you need your images sooner.

Yes, we understand that photography is an investment. We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking, with the remaining balance due on the day of the session. For larger packages or milestone packages, we offer interest-free payment plans. Please contact us to discuss options that work for your budget.
