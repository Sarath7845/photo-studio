Our Services

Discover our specialized photography services designed to capture the most precious moments of your baby's early days.

Capturing Precious Moments

At Little Wonders, we specialize in creating timeless images that tell the story of your family's most precious moments. Our professional photographers are trained in newborn safety and handling, ensuring your baby's comfort throughout the session. We offer a range of photography services tailored to different stages of your baby's early life.

Newborn Photography

Our newborn photography sessions are designed to capture the delicate features and peaceful moments of your baby's first days. These sessions are best scheduled within the first two weeks of life when babies are sleepier and more flexible for posing.

What to Expect:

  • 3-4 hour session in our warm, comfortable studio
  • A variety of poses, props, and setups
  • Family and sibling poses included
  • Professional editing and retouching
  • Online gallery with downloadable images

Maternity Photography

  • Multiple outfit changes with our maternity gown collection

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions about our photography services.

We recommend booking your newborn session during your second trimester to secure your spot. Newborn sessions are ideally scheduled within the first 5-14 days after birth when babies are sleepier and more flexible for posing. We understand that babies rarely arrive on their due date, so we keep our schedule flexible around your expected due date.

After booking, we'll send you a detailed preparation guide specific to your session type. For newborns, we recommend feeding your baby right before the session to help them sleep. For all sessions, we'll provide guidance on what to wear, what to bring, and how to prepare mentally for a successful and stress-free experience.

Newborn sessions typically last 3-4 hours to allow time for feeding, soothing, and posing. Maternity and family sessions usually take 1-2 hours. Milestone sessions for older babies (3+ months) typically last 1-1.5 hours. We never rush our sessions and always allow plenty of time for breaks, feeding, and comforting as needed.

You'll receive a sneak peek of 2-3 images within 48 hours of your session. The full gallery will be ready within 2-3 weeks. Each image is carefully hand-edited to ensure the highest quality. Once your gallery is ready, you'll receive a link to view, download, and order prints directly from your online gallery.

Yes, we have a wide selection of props, wraps, headbands, and outfits for newborns and babies. For maternity sessions, we have a collection of maternity gowns in various colors and styles. You're also welcome to bring your own special items to incorporate into the session. We'll discuss all options during your pre-session consultation.
